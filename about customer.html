ECOLY is offering a turnkey solution for Grid Tie Solution at defined premises.
As one of the leading Service providers of small to mid-scale solar photovoltaic (PV) systems throughout Pakistan, ECOLY provides a full array of solar options along with the latest inverter technology and large-scale Tubular / Lithium battery storage solutions. We utilize the latest CAD technology for individually craft solutions in any mix of rooftop, ground mount, single and dual-axis tracker, and/or canopy-based installations.

The project activities comprise of:
  • System Design & Engineering
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of complete Solar system on turnkey basis incl. Panels, Inverter, Power box, cable conduit fittings as defined in the Proposal
  • Project planning & controlling
  • All the technical support & documentation required for the project.
